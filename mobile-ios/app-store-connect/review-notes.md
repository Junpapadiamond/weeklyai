# App Review Notes

## What this app does

WeeklyAI is an AI product discovery app.  
Users can browse curated AI products, read summary insights, and save favorites locally.

## What this app does NOT do

- No UGC publishing
- No user account login
- No subscription or in-app purchase
- No push notifications in v1
- No AI chat feature in v1

## External links policy

External product websites are opened by iOS system Safari, not inside the app WebView.

## Data and privacy summary

- Local storage: language, theme, favorites
- Server logs: request metadata for reliability and abuse prevention
